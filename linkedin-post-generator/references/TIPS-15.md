# 15 Practical Tips

1. **Hook like headline** - "Me despidieron", "Nunca lo conté"
2. **Scene > opinion** - Show, don't tell
3. **Frame shift** - End with insight, not "poor me"
4. **Debate questions** - "What would you do?" not "Tell me I'm right"
5. **Searchable titles** - "7 reasons nobody contacts you"
6. **Specific steps** - "In banner: phrase + result + proof"
7. **Repeat variations** - Same pattern, different angle
8. **Mention scarcity** - "48h", "Only 10 spots"
9. **One action** - "Comment 'gift'" not follow+like+DM
10. **Concrete details** - Add numbers or scenes
11. **Land metaphors** - Metaphor + concrete version
12. **Name antagonist** - "The algorithm", "The culture"
13. **Strong hook** - 90% leave if hook doesn't grab
14. **CTA timing emotional** - Story > Landing > Question
15. **CTA timing technical** - Useful, direct CTA

## Example Using Tips
```
Hook: "Tomé esta decisión y molestó a todos"  (Tip 1)
Body: Específico con escena (Tip 2, 10)
Lesson: Insight, not complaint (Tip 3)
CTA: "¿Tú qué harías?" (Tip 4, 14)
```
